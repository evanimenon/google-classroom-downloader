Classroom File Downloader

Classroom File Downloader is a secure, read-only educational utility that allows students and teachers to download their own Google Classroom assignments and attached Google Drive files for offline access, backup, and personal archival.

What this app does

  • Lists your Google Classroom courses
  • Displays assignments and coursework
  • Allows downloading attached Drive files
  • Does not modify, delete, or share any data
